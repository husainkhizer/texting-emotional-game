We need to replace ALL existing dialogue and tone in the game.

The current messages feel:
- overly emotional
- too explicit
- unrealistic
- like low-quality fanfic
- characters are too cooperative and explain too much

This is NOT the direction.

---

NEW DIRECTION (CRITICAL)

The game is NOT about expressing feelings.

It is about:
→ how communication decisions change shared understanding under uncertainty

Every line should feel:
- restrained
- slightly indirect
- realistic
- high-signal, low-word-count
- written by people who are thinking, not narrating

---

WRITING RULES (STRICT)

1. No over-explaining emotions  
   ❌ “I felt like you didn’t care”  
   ✅ “That didn’t land the way I expected”

2. No clean resolution  
   Conversations should feel unfinished or slightly misaligned

3. The other person should apply pressure  
   They challenge, redirect, or narrow the conversation

4. Every line should either:
   - increase clarity
   - create ambiguity
   - build or reduce trust
   - shift alignment

5. Keep responses short  
   Most lines should be 3–8 words

6. Avoid therapy language completely

7. Avoid dramatic phrasing  
   No “I never stopped thinking about you” type lines

---

TARGET FEEL

This should feel like:
- two thoughtful people with incomplete information
- mild tension in every exchange
- subtle power shifts
- realistic texting cadence

---

EXAMPLE (REFERENCE QUALITY)

Them:  
You didn’t respond.

You:  
I saw it late.

Them:  
You always say that.

You:  
That’s fair.

Them:  
So what happened.

You:  
I didn’t prioritize it.

Them:  
…right

---

Another example:

Them:  
Does this still make sense?

You:  
Depends what you think we agreed on.

Them:  
Same as before?

You:  
Not exactly.

---

Another example:

Them:  
So what is this.

You:  
Something I didn’t say earlier.

Them:  
Then say it.

STYLE SUMMARY

- minimal
- precise
- slightly uncomfortable
- no emotional monologues
- no “perfect” communication
- allow misinterpretation
